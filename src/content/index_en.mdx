# Vesper Vei

This is my personal corner on the web. I use it to publish notes, essays, and small experiments that are worth keeping around.

The site stays lightweight and calm, with the duck aesthetic preserved, while the content turns fully personal.

## What you'll find here

- Notes on things I am learning or building
- Longer writing when a topic deserves more room
- Small digital experiments, sketches, and ideas in progress
- A growing archive that reflects how I think and work

## About this blog

This blog is now shaped around my own work and interests rather than the original starter content.

I want it to remain simple to browse, pleasant to read, and flexible enough to hold both polished posts and unfinished thoughts.

## Welcome

Thanks for visiting. More personal writing will live here from now on.
