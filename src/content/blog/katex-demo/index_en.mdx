This post is a quick demo to verify that KaTeX rendering works correctly.

## Inline math

Einstein's mass-energy equivalence: $E = mc^2$.

For $a \ne 0$, the quadratic equation $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$ has solutions $x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$.

## Display math

The Gaussian summation formula:

$$
\sum_{i=1}^{n} i = \frac{n(n+1)}{2}
$$

Euler's identity:

$$
e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0
$$

## Matrix

$$
A =
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3 \\
4 & 5 & 6 \\
7 & 8 & 9
\end{bmatrix}
$$

## Piecewise function

$$
f(x) =
\begin{cases}
x^2, & x \ge 0 \\
-x, & x < 0
\end{cases}
$$

## Integral and limit

$$
\int_0^1 x^2\,dx = \frac{1}{3}
$$

$$
\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x} = 1
$$
